海蒂和爷爷

Heidi

剧情简介:


Heidi, a five-year-old orphan, is living with her grandfather in the Swiss mountains. Her happiness ends when her aunt Dete takes Heidi to Frankfurt, where she is to help take care of a sickly child,Klara.
海蒂，一个五岁的孤儿，和她的爷爷住在瑞士山区。当她的姑姑德特带海蒂去法兰克福，帮助照顾一个多病的孩子克拉拉时，她的幸福曲折故事就此开始。
